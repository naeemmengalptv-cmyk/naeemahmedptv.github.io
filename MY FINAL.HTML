About

I create clear, engaging visuals for TV screens — from channel IDs and lower-thirds to full virtual sets and cinematic title sequences. I currently serve as a Graphic Designer in the IT Department at PTV Quetta Center. I joined PTV in 2007 and have delivered thousands of graphics for news, entertainment, children’s shows, stage programs and documentaries.

Beyond broadcast, I produce short films and special effects, and I enjoy sharing positive, human-centered stories inspired by Rumi and Osho.
